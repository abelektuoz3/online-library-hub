Frequently asked questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How do I borrow a book?

+

To borrow a book, first login to your account, then go to the Catalog page, find the book you want, and click "Reserve". You can also visit the library in person with your student ID.

Are e-books available online?

+

Yes! Many of our resources are available as e-books. Look for the "E-Book" label in the catalog. You can access them immediately after reserving.

What are the library hours?

+

Monday-Friday: 8:00 AM - 9:00 PM
Saturday: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM
Sunday: Closed

How do I reserve a study room?

+

Go to the Reserve page, select your desired date and time, and choose a study room from the dropdown menu. Study rooms can be reserved for up to 3 hours per day.

What is the late return policy?

+

Books returned late incur a $0.50 per day fine. E-books are automatically returned on the due date with no late fees.

Can I access resources on my phone?

+

Yes! Our website is fully responsive and works on all devices. You can browse, reserve, and read e-books from your phone or tablet.
